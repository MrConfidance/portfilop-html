The Best Movies According to Gerts

My Top TV Series of All Time

See

One of the most intriguing series I've ever seen.
It tells the story of a post-apocalyptic world where every human on the planet has lost the ability to see.
They have been living like this for hundreds of years until the first child is born with the ability to see.

The Boys

Not much to say about it, but it's funny.
It tells a story about a world with real superheroes, but the joke is that superheroes are just like normal people.
In reality, if there were any superheroes in our world, they would be something like them. Must-watch!

The Foundation

The story is set in the galaxy 20,500 years from now.
There are skyscrapers hundreds of kilometers high, spaceships, inhabited planets, and many other exciting things.
This is one of my favorite series of all time, and the second season has just been released!
